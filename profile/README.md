## 🚀 Overview
Background Remover for Windows uses AI to detect the subject of a photo and separate it from everything behind it, so you can delete a busy or distracting background in a single click. Whether you need a clean product shot, a portrait, or a graphic ready to drop onto a new layout, this image background remover isolates the subject and lets you export a transparent PNG or swap in a solid white background. It removes the tedious manual masking that used to take minutes per image, turning it into an automatic step. A free version lets you try the core cutout before you commit.

## ✨ Features
- **AI auto-cutout** — Detects the main subject and removes the background automatically, no manual selection or tracing required.
- **Transparent PNG export** — Save your result as a PNG background remover output with a fully transparent alpha channel, ready for design tools.
- **White background replacement** — Instantly place your cutout on a clean white backdrop for catalog-ready product and profile images.
- **Batch processing** — Run this photo background remover across many images at once to keep large sets consistent.
- **Edge refinement** — Fine-tune around hair and fine detail so the removed edge looks natural, not cut with scissors.
